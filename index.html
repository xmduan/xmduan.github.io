About me

Welcome to my personal website. I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at UC Santa Barbara. I am honored to have a chance to work with Prof. Francesco Bullo. Before coming to UCSB, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Automation from the Beijing Institute of Technology in 2013 and my Master's degree in Control Science and Engineering from Zhejiang University in 2016 under the supervision of Prof. Jiming Chen. My current research focuses on the design of stochastic surveillance algorithms for robotic systems.
